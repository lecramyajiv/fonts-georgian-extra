The fonts were created as part of GeoLab’s course “Basics of Typeface Design and
Font Engineering." In 2016, the course was held with the support
of the #TsereKartulad #WriteInGeorgian campaign and GeoLab. As part of the course,
a number of Georgian fonts found in old Georgian books and documents were
restored and digitized. About 20 designers and their mentors worked on
this project for a long time. As a result, anyone who wishes can
download these font files and use them as they wish.

The Fonts are freeware.
